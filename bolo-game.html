BoloBall

A fan remake of the 1992 classic by DDR, with art from Jetrel.

Version 1.0 (Feburary 2026)

Rules

  • To play, choose a ball to roll down the playfield.
  • The ball is scored based on which row it lands on. Each row is worth 12 points.
  • A bonus of 24 points is awarded if the ball reaches the final row.
  • The round ends when no more moves can be made by either player.
  • All grey blocks are removed at the end of the round. Any balls resting on them roll down and are scored again.
  • When all rounds have been played, the game ends and the team with the highest score wins.

Thanks for playing!
